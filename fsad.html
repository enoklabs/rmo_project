Full Sail Ad

For this project, we had to recreate a Full Sail University commercial. This was our basic introduction to video editing, using the provided videos. Everything was created using Apple's iMovie software so I had to trim the videos to the appropriate times, add different transitioning effects and fade sound track and volume adjusting with other sound effects.
